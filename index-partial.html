Professional profile

Since September 2015 I have started to work @ Frog, as a visual designer. In July 2015 I graduated with honors from NABA (Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti - Milano) gaining a BA in Graphic Design. My previous education includes graduating in 2009 as a photographer and completing a three-year course in Gaphic Design at Scuola Comics in Florence. In recent years I have worked for various companies based in Florence and Milan.
